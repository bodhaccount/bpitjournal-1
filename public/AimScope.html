Aim and Scope

BBIJTM AIM
BODH BPIT International Journal of Technology and Management (BBIJTM) , an International, Open Access, peer reviewed, Biannual Research Journal, being published since 2015 by Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology (BPIT) Delhi affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, New Delhi.

    The objectives of the journal are as:
  • To investigate the most recent research developments, as well as the publication and widespread electronic dissemination of innovative and substantial research.
  • Obtaining and soliciting high-quality original research papers.
  • To strengthen readers' research knowledge and to serve as an effective platform for the promotion of scientific and managerial exchange of knowledge and recent developments among active researchers, industry personage, engineers, scientists, and students.
  • To contribute to the progress in research by providing a high-quality online platform for publishing original research works.

BBIJTM SCOPE

Welcomes original and contemporary research in the broad area of Technology and Management, both conceptual and empirical. Authors are invited to submit their research contributions for the forthcoming edition. The Journal Publishes in both print and online version. The journal publishes research articles in Technology, and Management on theoretical foundations, computational, practical techniques, application, and implementations.



The journal is currently accepting research paper for publication in the following fields of :

  • Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Computer Networks, Data Mining, Information & Network Security, Natural Language Processing, Soft Computing, Software Engineering.
  • Applied Sciences: Atomic, Molecular & Optical Physics, Condensed Matter Physics Corrosion Engineering, Differential Equation, Electro Chemistry, Polymers, Materials Sciences, Theoretical Particle Physics, Mathematical Modelling.
  • Electrical: Electrical Machines & Drives, Energy Systems, Energy and Power Technologies, Computer Vision, Image Processing, Power Systems, Renewable Energy Sources.
  • Electronics and Communication: Signal Processing, Wireless Communication, VLSI & Microelectronics, Mobile Computing, RF & Microwave Engineering, Robotics & Automation, Internet of Things, Remote Sensing, Smart Cities Nano Materials, Computer Vision, Biomedical Imaging, Control Systems, Controls & Dynamics of Complex Systems.
  • Management: Organizational Innovation, Green Technologies, Business Continuity Cross Cultural Management, Corporate Sustainability, Total Quality Management, Technology Management, Change Management, Entrepreneurship, Business & Ethics E-commerce, Role of SMEs in National Economies, Emerging Role of Women in Business, Risk Management, Knowledge Management, Strategy, Systems and Governance, Knowledge Life Cycles in Organizations, Knowledge Acquisition, Transfer Processes & Reuse in Organizations, Transformational Leadership, Business Process, Reengineering, Corporate Governance, Accounting & Taxation, Process Innovation Enterprise Resource Planning, Benchmarking, Rural Management, Crisis Management, Business Intelligence, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Strategic Management, Corporate Social Responsibility, Environmental Management, Intellectual Property Rights Tourism and Hospitality Management, Healthcare Management, International Management Practices, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Operations Management, Finance, Global Business, Economics, Management Science and Statistics, Digital Business, Business Law, Finance, Marketing, Human Resource management, E-commerce, Operations, Supply Chain management, Economics, International business.

All submitted manuscripts are subjected to a double-blind review process. There is no fee for manuscript publication in this journal. Submissions must be well-organized, written in English, and describe original work that has not been previously published or is currently under consideration by other journals or conferences.
