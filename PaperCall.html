Call for Research Paper


BPIT is pleased to announce the launching of its first bi-annual multi-disciplinary journal – Bodh: BPIT International Journal of Technology and Management in May-June, 2015 . The journal will help to build emerging fields of study and enhance communication within existing research fraternity in technology and management. The journal aims at publishing quality research papers that are theoretical, empirical and application oriented and promotes the link between Technology and Management.
It will help to endorse all Innovations in Technology and Management to enable researchers and academicians integrate their ideas and contribute to the knowledge base.
BPIT International Journal of Technology and Management welcomes authors/research scholars for publication of high quality papers in different areas of technology and management. The papers will be published with an ISSN number.

    Instructions to Authors
  • The author should submit a full length paper in accordance with the given format including abstract, tables, figures, results, conclusion and references.
  • The paper should be sent by e-mail to bodhbpitijtm@gmail.com and bodhbpitijtm@bpitindia.com.
  • The paper should be original and unpublished and should not be currently under consideration for any other event.
  • An acknowledgment mail will be sent within a day. This mail will be a confirmation from our side that your paper has been received successfully.
  • As per the norms, the paper submitted will undergo an expert peer review process for short listing.
